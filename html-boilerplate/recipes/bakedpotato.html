Microwave Baked Potato

Description

If you want the yummy taste of a slowly baked potato but aren't patient enough, or don't have the know-how to bake it in an oven, this is for you. Give it 12 minutes and get a mouthwatering, taste bud-tingling treat.

Ingredients

  • 1 Potato
  • Any toppings you can think of

Steps

  1. Scrub your potato under running water, prick it with a fork, then place it on a microwave-safe plate.
  2. Microwave for about 5 minutes, flip it over, and microwave it for about 5 more minutes.
  3. Let the potato cool for just a few minutes, until it's safe to handle.
  4. Cut the potato lengthwise so that it opens and forms a natural bowl.
  5. Add any topping.
  6. Serve!
