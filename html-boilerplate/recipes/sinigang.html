Sinigang Tilapia

Description

A sour soup that is tasty full of savory, citric flavor. This variation uses tilapia fish as its protien, but any fish could work.

Ingredients

  • 0.5 pound tilapia fillet, cut into chunks
  • 1 small bok choy head, chopped
  • 2 medium tomatoes, cut into chunks
  • 1 cup daikon radish, thinly sliced
  • 0.25 cup tamarind paste
  • 3 cups water

Steps

  1. In a medium pot, combine the tilapia, bok choy, tomatoes and radish.
  2. Stir together the tamarind paste and water; pour into the pot.
  3. Bring to a boil, and cook for 5 minutes, or just until the fish is cooked through.
  4. Tip: Even frozen fish will cook in less than 10 minutes. Do not over cook or else the fish will fall apart.
  5. Ladle into bowls.
  6. Serve!
